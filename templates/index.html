About Rain Seekers

  • Rain Seekers is designed to turn NASA’s Earth observation data into a personalized, interactive experience for anyone planning outdoor activities. Our web dashboard lets users explore the likelihood of weather conditions — including heat, cold, rain, wind, and humidity — at a specific location and date of their choice.

  • Users can provide their desired location by typing a place name, selecting an area on a map. The dashboard then presents probabilities and trends using a variety of visualizations, including time series, maps, and summary charts. Clear explanations accompany each visualization, making complex climate data easy to understand and apply.

  • For those who want to analyze the data further, Rain Seekers also allows users to download the output in JSON format, including metadata and source references. By combining interactive visuals, probability insights, and downloadable data, Rain Seekers empowers users to plan smarter and enjoy outdoor experiences with confidence.
